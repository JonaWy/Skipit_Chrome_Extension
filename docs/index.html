FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SkipIt.

What streaming services does SkipIt support?

SkipIt supports Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, Crunchyroll, Apple TV+, and Paramount+ for automatic intro and recap skipping. The video speed control feature works on any website with HTML5 video players, including YouTube, Vimeo, and educational platforms.

Is SkipIt free to use?

Yes, SkipIt is completely free. You can install it from the Chrome Web Store at no cost. There are no premium features, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

How do I change video playback speed with SkipIt?

There are three ways to control playback speed:

  • Popup slider: Click the SkipIt icon and drag the slider
  • Preset buttons: Click any preset (1.0x, 1.25x, 1.5x, etc.)
  • Keyboard shortcuts: Press + to increase, - to decrease speed, or 1-8 to select a preset
Does SkipIt collect my data?

No, SkipIt does not collect any personal data. All settings and statistics are stored locally on your device or synced privately via your Chrome profile. There are no analytics, third-party trackers, or external data transmission.

How does the auto-skip feature work?

SkipIt automatically detects skip buttons on streaming platforms (like "Skip Intro", "Skip Recap", or "Skip Credits") and clicks them for you. It supports multiple languages and works silently in the background while you watch your content.

Does SkipIt work on YouTube?

Yes! While YouTube has built-in speed controls, SkipIt offers more granular control with speeds up to 4x (vs YouTube's 2x limit) and convenient keyboard shortcuts. The auto-skip feature is designed for streaming platforms, not YouTube ads.
